Goat Facts

How do goats breed?

Goats are mammals. They breed just about the same as other mammals. Male goats are called billy as in "billy goat" while females are called nannies "nanny goats".

Why is Goat our mascot?

Goats throughtout history are known to be the symbol of being independent and willing to surpass any obstacle in its way to suceed.

What do Goats symbolize?

Goats symbolize sure-footedness, fertility, independence, tranquility, ambition, aspiration, disobedience and of course faith. Goats are also strong willed and fierce.
